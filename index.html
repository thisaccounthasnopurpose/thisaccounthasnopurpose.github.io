What is Cryptojacking? (And Why This Isn't It)

Cryptojacking is the malicious and unauthorized use of someone's computer to mine cryptocurrency. This website is an ethical alternative, using a transparent, consent-based approach to demonstrate how browser-based mining works. Mining only starts when you consent, and you have full control over the process.
